Privacy Policy for Twofold

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Lan Mu ("we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile game Twofold (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not ask you for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal contact information.

The App uses the following third-party services provided by Google, which automatically collect limited technical and usage data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous or pseudonymous usage data such as which screens are viewed, in-game actions (for example, games played and scores reached), session length, approximate region, device model, operating system version, language, and a mobile advertising identifier / app instance identifier. This helps us understand how players use the App and improve it.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports and diagnostic information (such as device state, operating system version, and the technical stack trace at the time of a crash) so we can find and fix bugs.

We do not collect the contents of your files, contacts, photos, location (GPS), microphone, or camera.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data only to:

  • operate, maintain, and improve the App;
  • understand gameplay trends and balance game difficulty;
  • diagnose crashes and fix technical problems.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Data Sharing

The technical and usage data described above is processed by Google as our service provider. You can learn how Google uses data at https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

4. Data Retention

Analytics and crash data is retained according to the standard retention periods of the Firebase services and is used only for the purposes stated above.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device's system settings (Settings › Privacy / Google › Ads).
  • You can stop all further data collection at any time by uninstalling the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes may also be announced within the App.
